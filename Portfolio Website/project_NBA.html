NBA analysis

In my ongoing NBA analysis project, I explore the data from the 2021-2022 season to uncover valuable insights and trends. Utilizing Jupyter Notebook and popular libraries such as NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn, Plotly, and Chloropleth, I analyze the data to gain a comprehensive understanding.

Thorough data collection and preprocessing ensure the accuracy and quality of the dataset. By applying statistical techniques and visualizations, I aim to reveal patterns and provide insights into various aspects of the season, including player performance, team dynamics, and game strategies.

To enhance accessibility, I create intuitive charts, graphs, and interactive visuals that facilitate a better understanding of the findings. The project is a work in progress, with ongoing analysis and updates to the data.

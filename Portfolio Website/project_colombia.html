Colombian Data Science Project

In my data science project on Colombia, I analyze demographic data and compare cities and regions across Colombia. By exploring data sets and applying statistical techniques, I uncover valuable insights and trends about population characteristics and distribution.

Through meticulous data collection, preprocessing and analysis, I have gained a comprehensive understanding of the demographics and population dynamics in the different cities and regions of Colombia. By visualizing the data with interactive charts, graphs, and images, I present the results in a clear and informative manner.

In this project, I compare the population sizes of different cities and regions, explore the factors that influence population growth, and look at the population distribution across the country. Additionally, I analyze the demographic structure and identify any notable patterns or disparities.
